About ProjectHub

ProjectHub is a secure academic platform for final year students . It provides verified final year project materials, secure payments, and tools such as a topic generator to support students from proposal to completion.

All materials are reviewed and approved by administrators before publication. Payments are processed securely via Paystack, and downloads are protected using short-lived links and access control.
